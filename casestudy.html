Affinity Diagram

Affinity Takeaways The main pain point people express was that people make dog breeding into business and less about the importance of the dog wellbeing. 50/50% of the  people care if the other dog was purebred (pedigree). This drove us to unnderstand that our navigation had to address these categories and still remain enjoyable to use.

Problem Statement

We believe that pet owners have a lack of resources when finding someone to breed their dog. Additionally, that the market does not have a strong resource that owners can turn to for this goal.

With our app Sniff  we feel that we can help dog owners find matches for their dogs easier by connecting owners together who share the common goal of making healthy, meaningful, and lasting relationships.

Challenges

Every journey has its challenges.

1) Breeds can be very specific. Finding ways to include all the choices that come with breeding dogs was a challenge that involved some research.

2) We had to make sure we defined the casual owner who simply wants puppies and the serious who wants pure bred.

What the future of Sniff?

We had a great team with tons of ideas!!

1) add a playdate feature in the app for pet owners.

2) Don't just make the app that's exclusive for dogs, in the future we would like to add breeding sections for cats, lizards, or birds or whatever pet our users would like to breed!

3) to do collaborations with nutrition and dog supply companies to advertise in our app and provide discount to our users.
